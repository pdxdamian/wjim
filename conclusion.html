Results will be analyzed from each individual result. Key questions will be what confirm the hypothesis. Questions regarding the financial help and Opportunities that are offered to children looking to get more into motorsport. Similarities will be drawn from the various answers to see who are the people saying there are opportunities and who are saying there are none.

Significance and Conclusion

Looking into the youth is important for any sport, but one that has been neglected has been motorsport. There are few articles that look into the importance of go-karting and how these children are transferring to a higher level. Looking more into this part of motor racing will give a perspective on who are the children that are graduating to a higher level and who are being left behind and why. At the same time looking at who is left behind it can be investigated if children that struggle with financials are given assistance can they succeed.
